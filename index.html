Kellogg Photography Club


The mission of the Kellogg Photography Club is to provide an engaging, creative forum through which the Kellogg community can discover and experience the joy of photography. The KPC strives to hold multiple events each academic year where Kellogg students and others can learn about photography, benefit from the knowledge of other KPC members, and share their passion.
